Believers Church Medical College Hospital (BCMCH) is a 750-bed tertiary care hospital that has become one of India's most effective medical institutions.

It offers world-class affordable primary care for patients, as well as a full range of specialized care. It provides patients with well-equipped operation theaters, state-of-the-art laboratories, imaging facilities and excellent Critical Care Units.

The medical college is currently training more than 800 medical students, including future doctors, nurses and pharmacists.

The hospital helps almost 2,000 patients per day.

Recognition and Distinctions of BCMCH

  • World-class Infrastructure for all: The highest quality infrastructure means no compromises are made in care, regardless of socio-economic status.
  • Awards: BCMCH has won multiple global awards, such as being awarded the highest level competency in stroke response and care from the world stroke organization.
  • Quality Accreditation: BCMCH has the highest level NABH quality accreditation across multiple modalities, assuring an exceptional level of care.
  • International Partnerships: BCMCH has partnered with multiple international institutions such as George Washington University, Karlstad University, and BaylorScott&White Healthcare.

Bringing Hope and Healing

For more than 10 years, BCMCH has been fulfilling its vision of delivering hope and healing with the love of Christ. The hospital has been an anchor for ministry throughout Asia by conducting medical training and medical camps, which provide free medical care to people living in rural areas without access to any medical facilities.

Every year, hundreds of medical camps are held in communities without access to medical care. Millions of supplements and medications are distributed for free each year. More than one million women have received free health care training that will help their entire family, and 60,400 families have been taught the importance of proper sanitation.
