About Us

Africa 2035 is an initiative of GFA World, a Christian missions organization seeking to transform communities through Christ's love throughout the world.

More than 2 billion people have not heard of Christ's love.

Daily about 66,000 people die without hearing the Gospel once.

We want to change that.

Our passion is to share Christ's love with as many people as possible while there is still time.

You Can Be the Answer for Those Still Waiting

The opportunity is now. The Lord has established unprecedented open doors for us in Rwanda, providing a Bible college, our first congregation and a robust children’s ministry.

Our medical university and hospital in Rwanda is a major step for an expanded work across Africa offering advanced healthcare for those in need.

We plan to expand ministry to six other nations in Africa during the next few years and already have begun working in Liberia.

The Rwanda medical facilities are crucial for an expansive solid work to transforming communities across the continent.

Be a part of what God is doing in Africa.
