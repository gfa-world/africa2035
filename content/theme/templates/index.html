Transforming a continent through Christ-centered healthcare

We are building a hospital, but it's more than that.

It's about continental transformation.

Our goal is to take much-needed medical care + the Gospel to people across the continent of Africa who are without access to both. The hospital we're building in Rwanda will be an integral part of the ministry currently growing and thriving in the nation, and it will provide inroads across the continent for medical missions.

This rare and unprecedented opportunity started with conversations and a formal invitation from local communities and church leaders in Rwanda to replicate what's been done in Asia.

This is our vision.

100,000 communities

We want to see communities impacted for eternity, both physically and spiritually, in the name of Jesus Christ.

500,000 patients

We plan on helping over half a million patients receive world-class medical care in the first two years.

10,000 missionaries

Our long-term vision is to send missionaries into communities across the continent, planting churches that bring the healing hope of the Gospel to those in need.

How do we know this can happen?
Because we've done it before.

We've experienced the incredible life-changing power a hospital brings to a nation through the Believers Church Medical College Hospital (BCMCH) in India.

BCMCH, a 750-bed tertiary hospital that serves about 2,000 people daily, is one of the most trusted hospitals in India.

Quick facts about BCMCH:

  • 800 medical, nursing and pharmacology students
  • 3 million+ outpatients
  • 233,000+ inpatients
  • 76,000+ surgeries
  • 20 specialties
  • 20 super specialties
  • 55,000+ telehealth consultations
  • Numerous accreditations and awards, including 2024 NABH Reaccreditation and 2025 Diamond Status from the World Stroke Organization
