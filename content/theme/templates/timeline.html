See where we've been and where we're going!

2014
Believers Church Medical University Hospital established in South India.
2019
Senior church leaders from Rwanda approached us, asking if it was possible to build a similar, much-needed hospital in their home country.
2021
God moved on the heart of a supporter to provide the resources necessary to purchase the land where the medical hospital would be built. GFA World's ministry in Rwanda began by helping 50 children in dire need through the Child Sponsorship Program. Since then, the ministry has grown rapidly to include more than 2,000 children in five communities!
2022
Official groundbreaking to begin construction of the hospital
2025
Phase I of the Believers International Hospital is nearing completion.
2026
Phase II construction will begin on the medical University, staffing quarters and campus facilities. Anticipated inauguration of the hospital in May 2026
2027
Phase III of the Medical University will be completed. Medical University to have an enrollment of 500 students per year
2028
All three phases of construction completed: hospital, medical University, staffing quarters and advanced treatment centre.
2030
First class graduates, some graduates fill positions in Rwanda hospital, some go as medical missionaries to other African countries.
2031
First permanent clinics established in other countries. Also, first pastorally trained missionaries graduate from BEC Bible College in Rwanda to work alongside medical teams to establish churches.
2035
Expansion efforts will begin for another hospital and medical University in the western region of Africa (in addition to work in Asia).
